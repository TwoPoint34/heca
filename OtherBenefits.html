Other Promising Benefits

Expand our science-based services to better serve needs of people and the planet.

Fire: Implement firewise (fire-resistant) landscaping principles and choose firewise plant species, especially near buildings.

Ice: Although GSI is typically most effective in warmer temperatures, carefully-designed installations can still increase snow melt runoff retention, infiltration, and evaporation and thereby reduce icy sidewalks and driveways. HEcA can use high-resolution imagery to locate bioretention downstream of snow melt concentration and upstream of ice-prone surfaces such as driveways and sidewalks. We can also assess soils and runoff hydrology to determine if depaving and permeable paving would be high impact interventions.

Sustainable Home Energy: HEcA can perform a feasibility analysis to estimate home solar potential and to see if space requirements for geothermal may be met. We can also explore some of the potential financing options that may allow a homeowner to pay over time for the installations through the energy savings they provide. If the preliminary numbers crunch, we can then refer homeowners to trusted contractors for further assessment and installation.

As for metrics of success for HEcA's work, a client can qualitatively assess presence of winter ice in historically problematic locations. Firewise landscaping can be monitored over time to confirm that it retains its fire-resistant properties (e.g. not growing too large near buildings). Clients can also report back to us whether or not they ended up getting solar panels and/or geothermal installed and how happy they are with it.
