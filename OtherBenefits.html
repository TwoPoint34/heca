Other Promising Benefits

Expand our science-based offerings to better serve people and the planet.


Fire:
Implement fire-resistant landscaping principles and select appropriate plant species, especially near buildings.

Ice: Although GSI is typically most effective in warmer temperatures, carefully-designed installations can reduce snow melt runoff and thereby reduce icy sidewalks and driveways. NBHS can use high-resolution imagery to position green infrastructure downstream of concentrated snow melt and upstream of ice-prone surfaces. We can also analyze soils and runoff flows to determine if depaving or permeable paving are viable options.

Geothermal Heating/Cooling: NBHS can assess whether a property has the space required for a geothermal system and refer interested homeowners to licensed contractors for next steps (incl. long-term financing options).

Rooftop Solar: "Michigan has vast solar potential with more sunlight than Germany, the world’s leader (in) solar power."## NBHS can use aerial imagery and GIS to estimate rooftop solar energy potential then refer to trusted installers.

Real Estate Acquisition: Whether you're looking for farmland or your ultimate urban homestead, NBHS can assess a property's soils, zoning, hydrology, and more before you "bet the farm."

Metrics of Success:
     • Client observes less winter ice in historically problematic locations
     • Fire-resistant landscaping monitored over time to make sure it still poses minimal fire risk to nearby buildings
     • Clients reports back to us on their satisfaction and savings from solar panels and/or geothermal
     • Buyers of a property give feedback on the role that NBHS played in helping them find the right property
