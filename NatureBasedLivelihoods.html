Nature-based Livelihoods

Generate skilled, long-term work that cooperates with nature to solve pressing challenges.


The landscaping profession has long emphasized the domination of nature and creation of essentially sterile lawns. Fortunately, in recent years there has been a growing movement to thoughtfully allow nature back into our yards. NBHS specialists harness deep theoretical and applied ecological knowledge to maximize long-term positive impacts for people and the planet. With an increasingly complex external funding environment, our ability to generate positive impact relies only on the initiative of our clients.

Computers are great and all, but Nature has been efficiently automating processes for billions of years. Working with (rather than against) natural processes can radically minimize upfront and continuing costs such as heavy equipment, large work vehicles, and fuel needs. This reduction is not only great for the planet but also greatly reduces the financial barriers required for aspiring professionals to enter this line of work. Deep ecological understanding combined with ergonomic labor practices ensures that our specialists stay healthy, happy, efficient, and actively learning throughout their careers. NBHS helps create small businesses and employment that will never be outsourced or replaced by robots.

Metrics of Success: • Total number of NBHS practitioners • Average earnings • Work Satisfaction
