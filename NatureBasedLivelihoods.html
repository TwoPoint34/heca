Nature-based Livelihoods

How to Create Nature-based Livelihoods

     • Sell ecological property services that are in high-demand
     • Teach NBHS specialists to work with rather than against ecological processes
     • Train specialists in rational usage of high tech tools and data (e.g. LiDAR)

Success can be tracked through: total number of NBHS practitioners; earnings and margins; job satisfaction; barriers to entry; ergonomics


A Closer Look...
Home landscaping has long emphasized the domination of Nature and creation of essentially sterile lawns. NBHS brings the best of Nature back to your yard while creating skilled, dignified, healthy livelihoods that rely only on the vision of our clients rather than on scarce external funding. NBHS values life-long learning, knowledge-sharing, selfless service, and fun. Nature has been efficiently automating ecological processes for billions of years, and a deep understanding of these processes added to GIS analysis and field-proven techniques can radically minimize landscaping costs and carbon emissions by essentially eliminating reliance on heavy equipment, digging, and fossil fuels. Costs and labor are so reduced, in fact, that a motivated individual can embark on a rewarding career with only a bicycle or bus fare and a few hand tools. Critically, it is extremely unlikely that these livelihoods will ever be outsourced or replaced by robots. Whether or not people who work with NBHS ultimately choose it as a career, they will become vastly more effective land stewards wherever they live and however they earn a living.
