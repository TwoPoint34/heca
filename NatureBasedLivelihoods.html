Nature-based Livelihoods

Nature-bubbed Livelihoods

Generate skilled, long-term work that cooperates with nature to solve pressing challenges.


Home landscaping has long relied on fossil fuel power to dominate nature and create more-or-less sterile lawns. Fortunately, there has been a growing movement in recent years to allow nature back into our yards. Ecological understanding and long-term planning are slowly beginning to find their way back into our landscape management decisions. HEcA specialists bring deep theoretical and hands-on practical knowledge to maximize positive impacts for people and the planet. With an increasingly complex external funding environment, HEcA's impact relies only on the vision and initiative of our homeowner clients.

Computers are great and all, but Nature has been efficiently automating processes for billions of years. Working with rather than against natural processes can radically minimize upfront and continuing costs such as heavy equipment, large work vehicles, and fuel needs. This reduction is not only great for the planet but also greatly reduces the financial barriers required for aspiring specialists to enter this line of work. Deep ecological understanding combined with ergonomic labor practices ensures that workers stay healthy, happy, and efficient over the long-term. The skilled, fine motor, case-by-case... local and unlikely to be replaced by robots... choose employment or start their own business... essentially limitless growth potential... HEcA's success at creating such livelihoods can be measured through the number of such practitioners, their average earnings, and their satisfaction with their work.
