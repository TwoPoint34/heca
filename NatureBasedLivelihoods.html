Nature-based Livelihoods

Generate skilled, long-term work that cooperates with nature to solve pressing challenges.

Home landscaping has long relied on fossil fuel power to dominate nature and create more-or-less sterile lawns. Fortunately, there has been a growing movement in recent years to allow nature back into our yards. Ecological understanding and long-term planning are slowly beginning to find their way back into our landscape management decisions. HEcA specialists bring deep theoretical and hands-on practical knowledge to maximize positive impacts for people and the planet. With an increasingly complex external funding environment, HEcA's impact relies only on the vision and initiative of our homeowner clients. Nature has billions of years of experience in efficiently automating processes. unlikely to be replaced by robots.
