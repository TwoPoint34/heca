Grow Food

A fresh, nutritious, local buffet for you (and for wildlife).


We analyze sun, soil, and water factors to select appropriate perennial plants that do not require re-planting each year, though some may benefit from pruning. Carefully chosen perennials out-compete grass and once established can be "weeded" simply by mowing or string-trimming around them. Our experts can even help clients identify and utilize edible plants that already exist on their property (i.e. foraging). Human and wildlife food can thereby be grown with minimal energy inputs and in a manner that integrates seamlessly with a homeowner's existing landscaping practices. Fruit tree success starts with genetics and location, and NBHS makes sure that the right tree goes in the right place. Clients can be satisfied with whatever fruit a hands-off approach will yield, or they can choose to improve production through a more intensive approach. While many homeowners will find more intensive fruit tree management to be a gratifying hobby, there are also exciting models in which fruit tree maintenance (e.g. pruning, harvest, clean-up) builds community, generates jobs, and is at least in part financed by the fruit harvest itself 9,10,11.

Bonus: We can extend our analysis of sun angles and shading to provide an estimate of rooftop solar energy potential.

Metrics of Success:
     • Count total number and type of species planted over time
     • Estimate production of food for humans and wildlife
     • Track clients' landscaping labor and costs before and after our involvement
     • Some clients may choose to estimate cost savings from home-grown food
