Grow Food

A fresh, nutritious, local buffet for you (and for wildlife).


While clients can certainly choose to plant whatever they want in their yards, NBHS has particular passion and expertise in edible plants. We analyze sun, soil, and water factors to select appropriate perennial plants that do not require re-planting each year, though some may benefit from pruning. Carefully chosen perennials out-compete grass and once established can be "weeded" simply by mowing or string-trimming around them. In this way, human and wildlife food can be grown with minimal energy inputs and in a manner that integrates seamlessly with a homeowner's existing landscaping practices. Our experts can even help clients identify and utilize edible plants that already exist on their property (i.e. foraging).
Bonus: We can extend our analysis of sun angles and shading to provide a map of rooftop solar energy potential.

Metrics of Success:
• Count total number and type of species planted over time
• Estimate production of food for humans and wildlife
• Track clients' landscaping labor and costs before and after our involvement
• Some clients may choose to estimate cost savings from home-grown food
