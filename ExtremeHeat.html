Reduce Risk from Extreme Heat

Michigan summers are getting hotter and power outages from thunderstorms could prove deadly for vulnerable populations.

Action: Pinpoint locations for tree and shrub planting to increase paved surface shading Action: Increase overall biomass to produce a general local cooling effect and contribute to climate change mitigation through increased above and below-ground carbon storage Action: Address specific outdoor comfort issues through shading and precision management of undesired radiant heating

Metrics of Success: -Track total number of trees and shrubs planted -Estimate overall biomass increase due to HEcA services -Perform qualitative (can be informal) survey on client outdoor comfort
